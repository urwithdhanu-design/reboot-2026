# Insurance products available on the Lloyds Banking Group / Reboot 2026 insurance platform

## Home insurance
Home insurance protects your buildings and contents. You can choose Buildings and Contents, Just Contents, or Just Buildings.
Key benefits: make claims online or by phone; choose your excess for most claims; no hidden charges.
Typical starting price: from £22 per month.
Product id: home-insurance. Category: Property.
Useful when you own or rent a home and need cover for damage, theft, or liability.

## Life insurance
Life insurance acts as a financial safety net for loved ones if you die during the policy term.
You choose a cover amount and term (for example 20 years). Smoker status affects premium.
Typical starting price: from £15 per month.
Product id: life-insurance. Category: Life.

## Critical illness cover
Pays a lump sum if you are diagnosed with a serious illness listed in the policy.
Can help with day-to-day costs while you recover.
Typical starting price: from £28 per month.
Product id: critical-illness. Category: Health.

## Car insurance
Vehicle cover for private cars. Choose Gold or Silver levels and manage cover online.
Typical cover types in quotes: Comprehensive, Third party fire & theft, Third party only.
Typical starting price: from £38 per month.
Product id: car-insurance. Category: Vehicle.

## Van insurance
Cover for vans used day-to-day or for work. Gold or Silver options.
Typical starting price: from £42 per month.
Product id: van-insurance. Category: Vehicle.

## Pet insurance
Cover for dogs and cats. Levels include Accident only, Accident & illness, and Lifetime.
Benefits: three cover levels, 24/7 vet advice via video, manage policy online.
Typical starting price: from £18 per month.
Product id: pet-insurance. Category: Pet.

## Health Plan
Hassle-free health cover from £18 a month in partnership with Vitality.
Available for ages 18 to 79. Essential plan pricing includes insurance premium tax.
Product id: health-plan. Category: Health.

## Income protection
Replaces a portion of your income if illness or injury stops you working.
Typical starting price: from £20 per month.
Product id: income-protection. Category: Life.

## Travel insurance
Cover for single trip, annual multi-trip, or backpacker travel.
Price depends on travellers and trip type.
Product id: travel-protect-plus. Category: Travel.

## How to get a quote
Log in, open the Product Marketplace, pick a product, and complete the quote wizard.
You can compare policies and quotes in the Compare tool under Policies.
