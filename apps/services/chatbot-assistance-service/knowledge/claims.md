# How to make and track insurance claims

## Starting a claim in the app
1. Log in to the insurance app.
2. Open Claims from the bottom navigation, or go to Policies and tap Make a claim.
3. Enter your policy reference (for example POL-HOME-001).
4. Choose a category such as Property, Vehicle, Health, Pet, Travel, Life, or Parametric.
5. Enter the amount claimed and a short description of what happened.
6. Tap Start a claim. You receive a claim id such as CLM-XXXXXXXX.

## Claim statuses
- submitted: claim received and waiting for review
- in_review: an agent is assessing the claim
- approved: claim accepted for payment
- rejected: claim declined (you will be told why)
- paid: settlement completed

## Evidence that helps
Upload or keep ready: photos of damage, invoices or receipts, police crime reference (theft), veterinary reports (pet), medical letters (health/critical illness).

## Home insurance claims tips
For home claims, tell us number of claims in the last 5 years during quoting. Excess you chose affects how much you pay on a claim. You can usually claim online or by phone.

## Parametric claims
Some covers can pay automatically when a measured trigger is met (for example rainfall above a threshold).
The parametric claim service evaluates rules and can create a claim in the claims service without a long form.
If a parametric rule matches, status still appears under Claims.

## After approval
Approved claims may settle via the payment ledger and blockchain orchestrator settlement flow.
You can also ask support about proof of insurance and policy documents from Policies.

## Common claim questions
Q: How long does a claim take?
A: Simple claims can move from submitted to in_review quickly. Complex claims need evidence and may take longer.

Q: Can I cancel a claim?
A: Contact support with your claim id. You can also open Claims to check status.

Q: Do I need an active policy?
A: Yes. You need a policy reference linked to cover you purchased or quoted as paid.
